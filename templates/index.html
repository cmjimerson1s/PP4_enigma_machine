Our Story

Founded in 2009, EnigmaMachine has a rich history of pioneering the escape room phenomenon. As one of the earliest entrants into the industry, we have been at the forefront of shaping the immersive entertainment landscape. Our passion for creating unique and thrilling experiences led us to introduce the concept of escape rooms to a captivated audience. Inovaters at our core, we proudly have many record firsts.

  • The first escape room to use full immersive storytelling.
  • The first escape room to introduce live actors to the game.
  • The first escape room to be played by a President of the United States!
